Services That I'll Be Offering:

✔ Transcription

Making audio/video accessible to people with hearing difficulties is always appreciated. Transcription is the task of typing subtitles to whatever is heard in the audio/video. While there were massive developments with AI and speech-to-text recognition software, clients are always needing human ears to understand context, terminology, speaker voices, provide timestamps, maintain consistency, etc. Here, at PhonbireWorks, we offer that service.
