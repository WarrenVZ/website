What Are HTML Email Templates?

An HTML email template is a piece of markup code, carefully designed, and tested to work on most popular email clients. Email clients don't follow HTML and CSS rendering standards like websites do in web browsers. Email developers have to come up with fancy workarounds for compatibility across email clients, and are limited to old-school HTML and CSS to make modern designs. HTML email developers have to put these email templates through rigorous testing to make sure they render properly, and also check that they don't get flagged by spam filters.
