A Little About Myself And What I Do

Hi, I'm Warren Van Zuydam. I created this personal website mainly to show off my random skills, host my portfolio, blogs, etc. for either anyone looking to employ me full-time, or for any potential freelance clients or digital shop customers.

I'm a self-taught web developer, graphic designer, and a freelance general transcriptionist with over 3 years of experience. All the graphics, this website's looks, functions, the services I offer, the products I sell... All of this is done by me. This website is built entirely in plain HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. If you haven't noticed, I am a "DIY" type of guy, so I will do anything and everything I take interest in, as long as it involves a computer.

My main roles are transcription (for freelance work), and graphic design (for full-time employment, though I am thinking of offering this as a freelance service too). All my other "skills" are more hobbies than anything, but hey, if you like my hobbies, then I can make a plan to extend my services.

I am very versatile when it comes to computers. I can solve almost any problem, I can pick up programs very quickly, and I can hyperfocus on my tasks. I do struggle a lot with "shiny object syndrome", so my hobbies and interests will chop-and-change every week or month. I do however keep my knowledge on all my hobbies. One day I'm teaching myself Python coding, the next day, I'm sharpening on my vector graphic design skills, and the other day, I'm messing around with websites.

I am VERY strict when it comes to money and spending. I WILL find a way to teach myself something for free if I wish to pursue the interest. And this mindset is especially important in today's economy, where everything is becoming absurdly expensive. I have a "no paid proprietary software policy", so suites such as Adobe Creative Cloud (utter cancer) are out of the question. FOSS is the future... FOSS protects people in economic downturn... FOSS saves money... FOSS is accessible...

I hope somebody will take notice of my list of skills and interests, and perhaps find a way to make use of me in their business. As I've mentioned, I hope to either get hired as a freelancer, or better, a full-time employee. I don't really care what I do, but I need something to get my life going. For a long time I struggled to settle on one interest to become a master at it, in order to be able to turn it into a living. I am always asking "What if this...? What if that...?" I know times are tough, and everyone needs multiple streams of income, but we all have to specialize in something, even if it means teaching oneself at home, for free, instead of taking the financial risk of paying for potentially bogus education with a rigid, uncreative curriculum.

My Qualifications

I'm a qualified TEFL teacher (English teacher). I completed my 120-hour TEFL theoretical course, as well as my 20-hour practical course. I learned the fundamentals of managind a classroom, teaching students in fun and engaging ways, how to use Zoom, its whiteboard functionality, breakout rooms, etc. I am still open to this field, though the competition is worse than the previously mentioned field, and it's once again challenging for someone who is introverted.

I'm a general transcriptionist. I'm not really "qualified" in general transcription. This field is usually requires being self-taught, and it is often done in the form of freelance, not full-time employment, though there are some really loyal clients who stick around for many years. I receive audio/video from a client, then I type what is being said in the audio/video. This requires patience, the ability to tell different voices apart, proofread, and have a strict understanding of transcription style guides/standards. Even though AI has been disrupting the job market these past few years, human transcription is still a high-in-demand skill. If you need an audio/video transcribed, you'll be glad to hear that I actively offer the freelance service of general transcription. If you need anything transcribed, and you live in South Africa, then you can use the form to send me a message via email.
