Raster-To-Vector Graphic Tracing

I will take your low-quality logo, web graphic, etc., and turn it into a crisp, infinitly-scalable image, known as an "SVG" (Scalable Vector Graphic), so you can own a proper source image for web use, stickers, billboards, etc.. This is done by professionally tracing nearly everything by hand to avoid artifacts such as wobbly lines, islands, etc. that come with auto tracing functions. Humans will always be needed for tracing low-quality graphics, as they're not an algorithm blindly following pixels on a screen. Only humans can understand exactly what look the original image graphic was going for. I will include a free CMYK/printer-friendly version of your graphic if you need it printed.
